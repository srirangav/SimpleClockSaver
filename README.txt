SimpleClock.saver v1.0.1
By Sriranga Veeraraghavan <ranga@calalum.org>

SimpleClock.saver is a simple Swift-based digital clock screensaver
for MacOSX 

To install:

Click on SimpleClock.saver and follow the instructions in the System
Preferences

Supported MacOSX versions:

v. 1.0.0  - 10.11+
